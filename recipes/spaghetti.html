Clemenza's Spaghetti

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 3 garlic cloves
  • 1 28-ounce can of crushed tomatoes
  • 1 28-ounce can of diced tomatoes
  • 2 6-ounce cans tomato paste
  • 5 Italian sausages, grilled and sliced
  • 1 lb meatballs, cooked (we used 12 meatballs)
  • A splash of sweet red wine
  • 1⁄4 cup sugar

Steps

  1. In a large pot, heat the oil over medium to low heat.
  2. Add in your garlic and cook with the oil for 1-2 minutes being careful not to let it the garlic burn.
  3. Add in your cans of tomatoes and paste. Stir continuously until the sauce is smooth (about 7-10 minutes).
  4. Add in your (cooked) meats and stir until incorporated.
  5. Add a splash of wine and sugar to taste.
  6. Reduce the heat to medium low and let the sauce simmer for 20 minutes – stirring occasionally. (You just want it to simmer vs a full boil).
  7. Serve over your choice of pasta.
